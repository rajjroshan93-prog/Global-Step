Listening Practice: Test 1

Simak audio simulasi aksen penutur asli di bawah, lalu jawab pertanyaan dengan akurat.

Sesi Rekaman Percakapan:

1. The speaker initially planned to meet the consultant on Wednesday, but what was the final agreed day?
2. According to the audio guidelines, what is the strict restriction for Task 1 answers?

Analisis Kunci Jawaban (Mencegah Distractors):
• Nomor 1: (B) Thursday. Pembicara sempat menyebut hari Rabu (*Wednesday*), namun langsung meralatnya di akhir kalimat karena ada jadwal rapat mendadak. Ini adalah tipe jebakan *distractor* klasik di IELTS.
• Nomor 2: (B) Minimum of 150 words required. Aturan baku penulisan Task 1 mewajibkan tulisan minimal berisi 150 kata, jika kurang nilai kriteria *Task Achievement* akan turun drastis.
